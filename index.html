After discovering their parents' marriage is on the brink of ending, the Salazar siblings scheme to bring them back together in time for their 20th anniversary in a prequel to "Four Sisters and a Wedding." A beautiful, sweet movie. Some might find it predictable, but I enjoyed it. If you're in the mood to watch something heartwarming, romantic, and most importantly centered around family bonding, this movie is a great choice! ⭐⭐⭐
Siblings reunite when their father is diagnosed with cancer, and must settle old issues as they spend time together. One of the best drama I've seen so far. It was so emotional and at the same time gives lesson to anyone who watched it. It is Highly recommended. ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Seventeen-year-old Athena Dizon unwittingly plays a trick on resident heartthrob and bad boy Kenji de los Reyes. All of a sudden, she finds herself pretending to be his girlfriend to make an ex jealous; however, she falls in love with him. Overall, "She's Dating the Gangster" is a romantic comedy that delivers on its promises. While it may not be a groundbreaking film in terms of its genre, the movie is a feel-good story that is worth a watch for those who enjoy romance and comedy. ⭐⭐⭐⭐
